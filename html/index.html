Compact Compliance Infrastructure Protection Pool (CCIP)

Policy proposal for establishing a Compact Compliance Infrastructure Protection Pool in Lake Powell to protect Upper Basin water rights, ensure Colorado River Compact compliance (Article III(d)/(e)), and safeguard infrastructure at elevation 3510'. Focuses on exclusive Upper Basin use of tribal and stranded power water, retiming releases, sideboards against Lower Basin sales/speculation, and monetization options via controlled forbearance or USBR purchases.
